<Allon Hadaya 5/4/11>
	This control scheme intends to raise the controlability of the 	quadcopter by mapping sliders to axis velocities instead of throttle, roll, and pitch.
	throttle slider : vertical velocity
	roll slider, pitch slider : lateral velocity
	thumb coolie hat : lateral trim
	b7, b6 : yaw trim
</Allon Hadaya>
